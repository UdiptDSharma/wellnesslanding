Our Services

01

Yoga

Our yoga instructors are certified and experienced. They can motivate both beginners to take up yoga, and more experienced practitioners to maintain and improve their flexibility and posture.

02

Meditation

Our meditation workshops are open to everyone, from the uninitiated to experts. The instructors have years of experience practising and teaching high stress individuals guided meditation.

03

Gymnastics

Our instructors are among the best gymnasts in the country, with several accolades earned between them. They are certified to train beginners to take up gymnastic either as a hobby or a form of fitness.

04

Pranayaam

Our experts have years of experience and knowledge of pranayaam, and will be able to teach you the science behind the breaths. You will be guided through the process for a safe and effective session.
